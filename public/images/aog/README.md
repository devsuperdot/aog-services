# AOG Images

Place your petroleum industry images in this directory.

## Required Images

All images will automatically have a uniform grayscale filter applied with:
- `grayscale(100%)` - Full black and white
- `contrast(1.1)` - Slightly increased contrast
- `brightness(0.9)` - Slightly darkened for consistency

### 1. platform-hero.jpg
**Location:** Hero section (right side)
**Content:** Oil platform or petroleum facility
**Recommended size:** 1200x1600px (portrait)
**Subject:** Wide shot of platform, industrial equipment, or facility

### 2. workers-equipment.jpg
**Location:** Values section (left side)
**Content:** Workers or equipment in action
**Recommended size:** 1000x1250px (4:5 aspect ratio)
**Subject:** Close-up of workers, drilling equipment, or machinery

## Notes

- Images can be in color - they will be converted to black & white automatically
- Higher resolution is better for quality
- Portrait orientation works best
- The CSS filter creates a uniform look across all images
- Dark overlays are applied for text readability
